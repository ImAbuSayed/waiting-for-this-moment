[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
I can feel the fever rushing through my veins
Like a summer river running from the rains
Your eyes are pulling every single wall apart
I have been holding back the rhythm of my heart

[Verse 1]
The dust is settling on this quiet little street
And every shadow falls exactly at our feet
I drove across the county just to see your face
To lose myself completely in this perfect place

[Pre-Chorus]
The midnight air is heavy and the moon is low
I watch the fire light up every step we go
You tilt your head and suddenly the world goes blind
Leaving everything we used to be behind

[Chorus]
I have been waiting for this moment all my life
To feel you cutting through the silence like a knife
Your touch is burning like a slow and steady flame
And nothing in my universe will be the same
